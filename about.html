Mumbai's best hotel is close to the US Embassy.

The Hotel BKC Crown is close to the Jio Trade Center and the US Embassy. The hotel offers easy access to the city’s major business district on the Bandra Kurla Complex. It provides guests with furnished rooms equipped with modern conveniences.
Mumbai experiences nice temperatures all year round, with June and July seeing the heaviest rainfall. From April to May, the climate warms and gets more humid. The winter months of November to February are often cold and pleasant. View our Mumbai hotels as well.
